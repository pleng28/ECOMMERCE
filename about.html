The Shack

Process

We send our explorers to the most remote forests and islands around the world to collect samples from the most exotic plants. They pick their plants based on the guidence of local healers and document their traditional use carefully. Although many of our plants are undocumented, our researchers in Geneva will test them using our Xspectrometer which will islolate all the distinct chemical compounds within the plant and test them against test samples of all the known diseases at a rate of 128 tests per second.

Methodology

From first contact with tribal healers and exotic plants, all the way to the sales rep, we operate with seamless efficiency based on the Japanese concept of "kaizen". Our spirit of consistent self improvement has guided our product line to become a streamlined large scale operation. Our cost gains from our large scale efficiency can not be mirrored by our competitors because we are the first and largest coorporation in the exotic medicine market. Because of our experience, we are able to provide higher quality treatments at a fraction of the price compared to newcomers.

Beneficiaries

Many lives have benefited from our work. Our combination of traditional knowledge of healers all over the world has enabled us to cure curable diseases in more efficient manners and reveal new cures for previously uncurable diseases.

We value the health of all the citizens of the world because we beleive in the intrinsic value of human life. We especially our people, including our explorers, researchers, communities, and above all, our customers.
